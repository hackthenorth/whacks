Let's talk about VEX.

 

What is VEX?

VEX is a dedicated space for the innovative mind. A storefront and showcase every Sunday from 3pm – 7pm for entrepreneurs and creative artists. VEX provides an outlet for the student body to be exposed to their peer pioneers and be inspired.

For the rest of the week, VEX is a collaborative workspace for students to come and free their imagination in a cool, clean, and cozy environment made by students, for students.

 

What can we sell?

The VEX team makes decisions on products that entrepreneurs can and cannot sell based on the readiness of the venture, value that the venture can bring to the UM community and whether the venture is legally acceptable in the UM space.

How can I get involved?

If you are an entrepreneur, apply using our application. If you’re customer or investor please feel free to click here and use our contact form to get in touch with the team.
