FAQ

How can I get there?

Probably by plane. You will have to be dropped off in Toronto and either take a dogsled or snowmobile to the hack point. Buses will only take you so far.

Are we hacking on the ice?

Not quite––hacking will take place in the luxury igloo, overlooking the ice. However, the ice will be open for other activities. Feel free to go out in the snow too!

Who can come?

Any undergraduate student from anywhere in the world that is strong enough to brave the cold. Contact us if you don’t fit that description: We have a training program available.

How big can teams be?

Teams are capped at four students. Don’t worry if you don’t have a team before you show up––there will be time to meet other students and share your ideas.

Are there any rules for the hacks?

They can be anything! We just ask that you don’t continue existing projects or reuse parts of old projects. 3rd party APIs & libraries are totally fine, though!

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop and any other electronics you’ll need for the weekend along with some clean clothes, toiletries, snow shoes, and northern-rated outdoors wear.
