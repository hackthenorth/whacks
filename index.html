FAQ

How can I get there?

From many schools, we will provide buses free of charge. From anywhere else, we will provide a travel reimbursement of up to $200.

Are we hacking on the field?

Not quite––hacking will take place in the luxury seating area, overlooking the field. However, the field will be open for other activities ;)

Who can come?

Any undergraduate student from anywhere in the world. Contact us if you don’t fit that description: we admit some high schoolers and graduate students, on a case-by-case basis.

How big can teams be?

Teams are capped at four students. Don’t worry if you don’t have a team before you show up––there will be time to meet other students and share your ideas.

Are there any rules for the hacks?

They can be anything! We just ask that you don’t continue existing projects or reuse parts of old projects. 3rd party APIs & libraries are totally fine, though!

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop and any other electronics you’ll need for the weekend along with some clean clothes and toiletries.
